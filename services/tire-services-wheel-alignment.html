Overview

Proper tire maintenance ensures safety, comfort, and fuel efficiency. JC's Automotive offers tire rotation, balancing, mounting, and wheel alignment services. We'll help you choose the right tires for your driving needs and keep them in top shape.

What's included

  • Even tire wear extends tread life
  • Better handling and ride comfort
  • Improved fuel economy
  • Reduced risk of blowouts

How it works

1

Inspection & Pressure Check

We inspect tread depth, wear patterns, and adjust pressure.

2

Rotation & Balancing

We rotate tires to promote even wear and balance each wheel.

3

Precision Alignment

We adjust camber, caster, and toe to manufacturer specs.

Common questions

How often should I rotate my tires?

Every 5,000-7,500 miles, typically with an oil change.

What are signs I need an alignment?

Pulling to one side, uneven tire wear, or a crooked steering wheel when driving straight.
