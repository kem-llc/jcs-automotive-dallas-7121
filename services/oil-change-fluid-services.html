Oil Change & Fluid Services

Keep your engine healthy with regular oil changes and fluid checks.

Overview

Routine oil changes are the best way to extend engine life. At JC's Automotive, we use high-quality synthetic and conventional oils. We also check and top off all other fluids — coolant, brake fluid, transmission fluid, and more — to keep your car running smoothly.

What's included

  • Improved engine longevity and performance
  • Better fuel efficiency
  • Prevents costly engine damage
  • Complimentary multi-point inspection included

How it works

1

Drain & Replace Oil

We drain old oil and replace with the correct grade and filter.

2

Fluid Level Check

We inspect and top off all critical fluids.

3

We check belts, hoses, tires, and lights for your safety.

Common questions

How often should I get an oil change?

Most modern cars need oil every 5,000-7,500 miles. We'll recommend the best interval for your vehicle.

Do you use synthetic oil?

Yes, we offer synthetic and conventional options to match your manufacturer's specs.
